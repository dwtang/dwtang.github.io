About

September 2024: I have joined eBay as an applied researcher. I work on applying deep learning, large language models, and statistical methods to improve search systems.

Prior to joining eBay, I was a postdoctoral researcher at University of Southern California. I worked with Prof. Rahul Jain, Prof. Ashutosh Nayyar, and Prof. Pierluigi Nuzzo on reinforcement learning and multi-armed bandit algorithms. I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, where I also obtained M.S. in Mathematics and M.S. in ECE.

In my spare time I play and learn musical instruments, listen to music, and continue trying and failing at writing original songs that are half as interesting as my research.
